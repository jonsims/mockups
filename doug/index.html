About SARIMA

A professional home for the people behind the research.

SARIMA is a registered not-for-profit company that brings together research and innovation management practitioners to strengthen these disciplines and institutional capabilities across the SADC region.

It operates at an individual, institutional, national and international level, across the continuum from research, where new knowledge that can drive innovation is created, through to innovation, which translates that knowledge into spin-offs, products and services. SARIMA has anchored itself as a key role player in the SADC regional system of research and innovation: providing thought leadership and advocacy, a platform for best practice, and championing efforts to build capacity within institutions and for individual practitioners.

Behind it stands a vast network of strategic partners and funders within Africa and abroad, passionate volunteer committee members, and a small but dedicated operational and project team.

Professionalisation

Recognition that travels with you.

SARIMA developed competency frameworks for research management and innovation management, and partners with international peer bodies so practitioners can earn accredited professional status, recognised locally and internationally.

Entry

RAP

Research Administration Professional

New entrants, or practitioners with 3+ years in a research management role who do not yet meet RMP requirements.

R3 000 Africa · R6 000 outside Africa

Mid to advanced

RMP

Research Management Professional

Mid- to advanced-career research managers employed in a research management position (3 to 5 years).

R5 000 Africa · R10 000 outside Africa

Senior

SRMP

Senior Research Management Professional

Senior research managers serving in a leadership or strategic role (more than 5 years).

R8 000 Africa · R16 000 outside Africa

Accreditation body: International Professional Recognition Council (IPRC). Calls are announced by SARIMA and on the IPRC website. Fees exclude VAT.

Established

RTTP

Registered Technology Transfer Professional

For practitioners with the core competencies and an established track record of more than 3 years.

US $300

Aspiring

Candidate RTTP

Candidate Technology Transfer Professional

For new entrants (6 months and up) intending to apply for full RTTP status after 3 years.

US $50

Accreditation body: Alliance of Technology Transfer Professionals (ATTP). Apply at attp.global.

Online short courses

Six courses. 112 training points.

Accredited, digitised short courses that build skills and earn IPRC training points, usable when applying for, renewing or upgrading a professional designation. Complete all six and the IPRC issues a Certificate of Completion.

20 pts

Fundamentals of Research Management

A range of knowledge and experiences that promote the value of the research management profession.

20 pts

Research Grants & Contracts Management

How post-award management fits into the grant process, and how to run projects to budget and funder expectations.

20 pts

Research Ethics & Integrity

Promote, foster and support research ethics, integrity, compliance and responsible conduct.

12 pts

Intro to IP, Technology Transfer & Commercialisation

A foundation for managing intellectual property, technology transfer and commercialisation in a public research organisation.

20 pts

Research Programme Evaluation

Design, guide and implement effective programme evaluation with confidence.

20 pts

Gender in Science, Technology & Innovation

The mutual shaping of gender and STI in Africa's national systems of innovation.
