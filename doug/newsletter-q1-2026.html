New this quarter

Fact sheet: Introduction to intellectual property, technology transfer and commercialisation

A new fact sheet is out this March, laying the groundwork for practitioners new to IP management, the technology transfer process, and commercialisation in public research organisations. Download the PDF.

Professionalisation corner

Professional recognition calls are announced by SARIMA and on the IPRC website through the year. A reminder of the routes:

  • Research management (IPRC): RAP, RMP and SRMP designations, from new entrants to senior leadership.
  • Technology transfer (ATTP): RTTP for established practitioners, Candidate RTTP for new entrants.

Completing all six accredited online short courses earns 112 IPRC training points and a Certificate of Completion, usable when applying for, renewing or upgrading a designation.

Membership

2026 renewals are open. Individual membership is R 985.14 a year (excl. VAT), students R 493.44, and the three-year option locks the first-year rate for three years. Organisations can bundle 10, 15 or 20 memberships, with up to six free. Join or renew.

Looking ahead

The mid-year training calendar lands in the Q2 edition, building toward the Johannesburg conference: licensing and deal structuring, open research in practice, the accredited introduction to IP, technology transfer and commercialisation, and more.
